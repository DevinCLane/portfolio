Software Engineer

I'm a creative software engineer who has delivered successful full-stack web applications since 2019.

Since 2006, I've also been a creative team lead, musician, teacher, and writer. I've taught thousands of lessons to hundreds of students; written, produced, marketed several albums; booked and performed several tours.
